---
title: 'TLS handshake'
shortDefinition: 'The negotiated setup for encrypted channels: parameters, certificates, and session keys.'
updatedAt: '2025-02-01'
---

The handshake is where clients and servers agree on **versions**, **ciphers**, and **identity proofs** (certificates). Many “TLS bugs” are really configuration drift: names, chains, and clock skew.
