---
title: 'Indicator of compromise (IoC)'
shortDefinition: 'An observable artifact suggesting malicious activity, often brittle without context.'
updatedAt: '2025-02-18'
---

IoCs are the “fingerprints” of incidents: hashes, domains, IPs, registry keys. They are useful for **fast containment** when they are fresh and scoped.

The failure mode is fetishizing lists. Strong detection prefers **behavioral invariants** (what must happen for an attack to succeed) over endless feeds of disposable artifacts.
