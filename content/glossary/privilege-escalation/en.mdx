---
title: 'Privilege escalation'
shortDefinition: 'Moving from a lower-trust identity to a higher-trust identity within a system.'
updatedAt: '2025-02-10'
---

Privilege escalation is not only “root vs user.” It is any **identity boundary crossing** that expands what you can read, execute, or affect—especially in distributed systems where “admin” is not a single switch.

Good notes focus on **primitives**: what capability did you gain, and what did it unlock next?
