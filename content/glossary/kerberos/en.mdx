---
title: 'Kerberos'
shortDefinition: 'A network authentication protocol built on tickets, time, and trusted third parties (KDCs).'
updatedAt: '2025-01-25'
---

Kerberos is the backbone of identity in many Windows environments. Its failure modes are often mundane: **clocks**, **realm names**, **SPN mismatches**, and **service account behavior**.

When studying attacks, separate protocol mechanics from vendor-specific tooling.
