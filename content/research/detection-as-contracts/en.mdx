## Hypothesis

Most “false positives” are **category errors**: the rule assumes fields mean X, but the pipeline delivers Y on Tuesdays.

## Next steps

- pick one workflow (privilege change) and draft a one-page contract
- map SIEM fields to that contract explicitly
- measure drift across major version upgrades
